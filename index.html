About us

Manning agency Pacific Ocean is located in Mariupol, Ukraine. We are experience team which are available 24/7.
Main objective of the company is to select qualified Ukrainian seafarers of all ranks, free of any charge for them.
Our crew manning agency supplies our Clients with well-experienced and highly qualified marine specialists from single officer to full crew for Container Carriers, Bulk Carriers (Panamax, Cape Size, Live Stock etc.) , Coasters, Reefer, RoRo/PCC, Chemical Tankers and others, and we will be happy to expand our business having enough potential and facilities for that.
We provide a complete package of services, such as crew planning, travel management, and instruction of seafarers before boarding the vessel.
Our goal is to expand our business to the highest standards of service and to develope kind and beneficial relationships with our potential and existing principals, clients and employees.
We will be very glad to serve you on any matters, please do not hesitate to contact us.
